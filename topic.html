The Pros of Social Media:

1. Increased Moivation to Online Learning:

-With the presence of using social media as another way to learn. Students now have a wide variety of platforms to use for their studies. For example, youtube, google and etc.

2. Boosts Creative Element:

-While browsing on social media, some students may discover videos or articles that may increase their creative skills. Some talenst and skills may be discovered in the process that enriches their knowledge.

The Cons of Social Media:

1. Causes Distarction:

-Social media platforms are a big challenge for both students and teachers. Students tend to spend hours on social media, decerasing their study time.

1. Laziness:

- Since everything can be found in social media, students now spend less time using their textbooks. Considering that it is easier to gain data and information, students' reading habits decrease over time and their critical thinking skills also deplete.
